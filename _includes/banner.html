Applying the principles of boxing to life

Applying the skills learned in the boxing ring to everyday life to navigate their environment to build a better life.

Building our communities by example

Teaching our youth how to value themselves and the individuals within their communities through boxing and mentorship.

Building Self-Confidence, Self-Discipline and Self-Respect

Through the sport of boxing we build up our mentors confidence, discipline and respect for themselves and others.

Unanimous Decisions is a positive youth development juvenile diversion program. We promote positive activities to support essential growth, self- worth, quality relationships, values, and leadership. We assist youth in using critical thinking skills to get the best result out of any situation and enable them to become better students, make career choices, say no to violence, manage anger, resolve conflict, respond to bullying without force, say no to drugs and gangs, and manage relationships.
